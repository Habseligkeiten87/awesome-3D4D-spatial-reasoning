Three Interwoven Narratives

Cognitive Science to AI

From Craik's mental models and Tolman's cognitive maps to modern world models and latent representations. Classical cognitive science provides theoretical foundations for contemporary machine spatial intelligence.

CraikTolmanShepardWorld Models

2D to 3D to 4D Evolution

Technical progression from 2D image understanding (LISA) through 3D scene comprehension (MLLM-For3D) to dynamic 4D spatio-temporal reasoning. Each dimension adds spatial and temporal complexity.

LISAMLLM-For3D4D Dynamics

Shortcut Bias Critique

SURPRISE3D reveals fundamental gaps: models exploit semantic shortcuts and 2D cues rather than performing true 3D spatial reasoning. This challenges assumptions about machine spatial intelligence.
